The core debate is whether this strength justifies the share price. On the other hand, as of 2026-06-23 the stock traded at 87.1x trailing earnings, 50.3x sales, 217.8x free cash flow, and 66.0x EV/EBITDA, while insiders were heavy net sellers with no cluster buying support [89]. My synthesis is that Micron’s operating outlook is bullish, but the stock already discounts a large portion of that upside. That supports a constructive business view but a more cautious investment stance at the current price [87][89].
